Taba

Situated at the northern tip of the Gulf of Aqaba, Taba offers some of the most spectacular natural landscapes in Egypt. Renowned for its clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and stunning views, Taba is a paradise for those looking to explore underwater wonders or simply relax with breathtaking scenery. Don't miss its rich cultural sites like the ancient Saladin Citadel on Pharaoh's Island.🌟

Features:

  • Stunning coral reefs and marine life
  • Saladin Citadel on Pharaoh's Island
  • Ideal for diving, snorkeling, and beach relaxation
